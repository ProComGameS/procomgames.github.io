Frequently asked questions.

Can I help contributing data?

Sure, you can definitely contribute data! However, it's important to understand that the data you provide will undergo a verification process. This means the information won't appear instantly.

Why do some cities have special marks while others are missing them? Are there any priorities?

Cities receive new data with each new expansion. The priorities for expansions are determined by city popularity, data availability, and user contributions. This ensures that every expansion is both realistic and well-executed.

How do recommended places work?

Users can recommend places they think are worth visiting. These recommendations are based on factors such as price, quality, and popularity. If a place is truly worthwhile, it will eventually be displayed on the map.
